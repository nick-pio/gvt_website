Welcome to Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam

Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam - Commercial and Domestic asphalt specialist Contractors. A family-run business established over 47 years ago by our late father Mr. Glyn Rees.

With over 47 years of experience in the trade, we are a well-established provider of tarmacadam and resurfacing services to both commercial and domestic clients. Surfacing and maintenance work to car parks, public roadways, private domestic driveways. We are Local authority approved contractors and certified to supply and lay thin lay asphalt and macadam. We undertake all types of tarmac work and Resin. We can also hire out paver and roller machinery.

Local authority approved

We are local authority approved contractors, and certified to supply and lay thin layers of asphalt and tarmacadam.

24/7 services

We are open 24/7, which means you can rely on us to respond to your queries or serve you at any time of the day or night. Do not hesitate to call us to inquire about our services.
